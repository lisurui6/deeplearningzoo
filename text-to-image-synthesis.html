Text to Image Synthesis

Since it is possible for neural networks to generate a description given an image, text-to-image synthesis focus on the opposite problem of generating images based on given descriptions. Automatic synthesis of realistic images from text would be interesting and useful, as generated images may be useful to increase the dataset size in some applications.

Generative Adversarial Text to Image Synthesis

In this project, we introduce a novel deep architecture and GAN formulation, by Scott Reed et.al, to effectively bridge these advances in text and image modeling, translating visual concepts from characters to pixels. They demonstrate the capability of our model to generate plausible images of birds and flowers from detailed text descriptions.
