About

Deep learning sounds like really high-tech, while in fact it can be really easy and fun. We hope that this website can help people catch up with the frontier of deep learning research, quicker and better, and inspire people to come up with more cool deep learning stuffs. We aim to lower the entry barrier of deep learning, so that you don’t need to have a background in computer science or machine learning to get involved in deep learning. And even when you do have these backgrounds, you may still currently find it tedious to just get started. The current problems are:

  • It is tedious to read science papers and reproduce them from scratch. Sometimes you just want to know the abstract and experiment them.

  • It is tedious to install all the libraries and configure your PC correctly. It is time that should be better spent.

  • It is tedious to find the right dataset and pretrained models. Public datasets are scattered all over Internet and each pretrained model is suitable for only one deep learning frameworks.

If these are the problems that have been bothering you, this is the right place for you. Currently, we just launch a testing version, where we have selected 5 cool deep learning projects for you to try them out in our cloud. We will be launching as many projects and training modules as possible soon, please stay tuned!
