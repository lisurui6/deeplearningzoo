Convolutional Neural Networks

  • Image Recognition
  • Automated Colorization

Convolutional neural networks (CNN) is mostly used for images, as its connectivity pattens are inspire by human visual cortex. Recent years, CNN has been proven to be most effective to recognise objects in images, even surpass human accuracy. Here we choose 2 applications for CNN at the moment to illustrate the magic of CNN.

Recurrent Neural Networks

  • Image Captioning
  • Neural Storyteller

Recurrent neural networks (RNN) is great for analysing time series data (sentence, videos, audio, etc.), as its inputs and outputs are usually dynamic.

Generative Adversarial Networks

  • Text to Image Synthesis

Generative adversarial networks (GAN) is a methodology of using two neural networks competing against, with one acting as a classifier to determine whether the output of the other network looks real. GAN is currently the most exciting area of deep learning, showing great promises of its capability of generating realistic images.
