Generating Stories about images

When looking at an image, human can not only describe the scene but also, with the power of imagination, come up with fictional stories about the scene, although it can be challenging. To tackle this task with neural networks is a great examination their possible creativity.

Neural Storyteller

This experiment explores how to generate little romantic stroies about images. It combines recurrent neural networks, skip-thoughts vectors and other techniques to generate little story about images. its outputs are creative and often comedic. A RNN decoder is trained using 14 millions passages of romance novels. Each passage from a novel is mapped to a skip-thought vector, which is an embedding of thoughts into vectors representation. Afterwards, RNN is conditioned on skip-thought vector to generate the stories.
