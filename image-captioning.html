Image Captioning

A quick glance at an image is sufficient for a human to point out and describe an immense amount of details about the visual scene. For computers, it is more complicated, since the image description needs to accurate about objects in the scene, and understand the context of the scene.

Deep Visual-Semantic Alignments for Generating Image Descriptions

In this paper, they combins a CNN with a bidirectional recurrent neural network to generate natural image description. Bidirectional means that the network examine the sentence in both direction to decide whether it make sense..
