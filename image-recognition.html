Image Recognition

Image recognition has been a hot topic recent years due to the development of deep learning. One brenchmark competitiion is ImageNet object recognition, where Facebook, Microsoft, and Google have their killer deep neural networks that are more accurate than humans. However, here we will present a new neural networks, created by pjreddie, that is the fastest neural network for image recognition with 150 frame per second efficiency.

YOLO: Real-Time Object Detection

Other deep learning methods for image recognition often divide the task into two stage: localize potential objects, and recognition the object in the proposed regions. Such method is called R-CNN: region based CNN. It can achieve very high accuracy, however, it fails to achieve real-time efficiency. YOLO believes that humans only need to look at an image once to locate and recognise objects. Based on the principle of "you only look once", they construct an end-to-end neural network that is able to localise and recognise objects are the same time.
